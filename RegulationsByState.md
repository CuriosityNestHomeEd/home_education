<H1>Homeschooling Regulations by State</H1>

<b><i> I am not a lawyer. Please check with your local state rules and regulations, at minimum yearly,
to ensure that you are following the rules and regulations of the area in which you live. Please note,
this information is primarily information from the United States of America. Homeschooling is not
legal in all areas.</i></b>

<i>Also note, some of these resource links refuse connections outside of the US.</i>
